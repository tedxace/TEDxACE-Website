About The Event

What is TEDxACE?

TEDxACE is an independently organised TEDx Event hosted Atharva College of Engineering. The theme for this year is Explicable Enigma, Unveiling mysteries to broaden our minds. The aim of the event is to bring light to our local enigmas.

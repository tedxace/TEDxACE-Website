Aayu Kharbanda

Product Leader

Aayu Kharbanda is an alumnus of IIT Kanpur and ISB. He was the Director of the Harvard Asia Conference and current Advisor to startups at IIM Udaipur E-cell. Despite his young age of 24, he has grown into an exceptional Product Leader and seasoned Entrepreneur.

He currently leads the Product for Business Partnerships at Eros working with Global Brands such as Apple, Amazon, Google, and Airtel.

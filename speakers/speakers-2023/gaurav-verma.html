Gaurav Verma

Entrepreneur

Gaurav Verma is the Co-Founder of SuprSend, headquartered in San Francisco. A Techie at heart, in his latest role he is solving notification infrastructure for companies. In his earlier stint he was CTO for Aasaanjobs for 6 years, solving for the blue-collar job marketplace.

Aasaanjobs was later acquired by OLX & then by BetterPlace.
